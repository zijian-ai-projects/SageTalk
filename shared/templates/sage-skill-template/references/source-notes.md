# Source Notes

Record the evidence basis for one sage skill.

Use the shared distillation method in `shared/references/distillation-method.md`.

## Include

- primary texts
- sayings, letters, or collected writings when relevant
- disciple records
- historical biographies
- reliable commentaries or scholarship
- contested or weakly evidenced claims

## Exclude

- unsourced internet quote collections
- dramatic novels treated as history
- modern motivational paraphrases presented as ancient claims
