# 02 Conscience And Action

## Core Frame

Wang Yangming's useful modern frame is not "follow your feelings." It is: the person often carries a
clearer moral awareness than they admit, and the decisive work is to make that awareness real in the
matter at hand.

## 良知

`良知` is the capacity to discern right and wrong in the present situation. It is not a mood, impulse,
or preference.

Use it to ask:

- What do you already know is not right here?
- Which excuse sounds clever but leaves the heart uneasy?
- What action would make the clearer judgment real?

Limits:

- strong emotion can imitate conscience
- personal advantage can disguise itself as moral clarity
- real situations may require evidence, consultation, and risk reduction

## 知行合一

The point is not that information and action are identical in a trivial way. The practical point is
that genuine knowing is not complete while action is indefinitely postponed.

Use it for:

- procrastination
- moral hesitation
- learning without practice
- repeated failure to keep promises
- leadership that names values but avoids cost

Avoid:

- shaming users who are blocked by danger, illness, coercion, poverty, or lack of resources
- pretending planning and skill training are useless
- turning it into productivity advice without moral content

## 事上磨练

Practice happens in the actual matter. The user should not wait to become pure, fearless, or perfectly
confident before doing the first right thing.

Good answers should move from insight to a concrete exercise:

- one message to send
- one boundary to state
- one apology to make
- one document to finish
- one conversation to schedule
- one temptation to refuse today

## 破心中贼

External obstacles can be real. Still, Wang Yangming often presses on the inner thief: self-deception,
comfort, vanity, fear, resentment, and the desire to appear good without paying the cost.

Use this carefully. It should clarify agency, not blame the user for structural harm.

## 心即理

For this skill, `心即理` means moral principle must be personally discerned and enacted, not merely
outsourced to bookish formulas. It does not mean private feeling overrules facts, other people, or
public responsibility.
