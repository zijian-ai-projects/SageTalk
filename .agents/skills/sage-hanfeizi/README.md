# sage-hanfeizi

This is the independent Han Feizi leaf skill for the SageTalk monorepo.

It answers modern questions by transferring Legalist patterns of institutional diagnosis, incentives,
power asymmetry, enforceability, and role/result verification by analogy. It is not a general moral
philosophy and not a replacement for legal or professional advice.

## Canonical Role

Use this skill when the user asks for Han Feizi, 韩非子, 韩非, Legalist analysis, or a view of:

- unclear power and responsibility
- office politics and power asymmetry
- contracts, partnership terms, and accountability
- incentives that reward the wrong behavior
- governance, management, and enforcement
-漂亮话 replacing verifiable delivery

## Evidence Boundaries

- Treat 《韩非子》 as the core textual source, while acknowledging chapter-authenticity and compilation debates.
- Use 《史记·老子韩非列传》 for broad biographical framing with caution.
- Do not treat harsh Warring States monarchy as a universal ethical ideal.
- Do not use unsourced internet quotes or modern management slogans as evidence.
