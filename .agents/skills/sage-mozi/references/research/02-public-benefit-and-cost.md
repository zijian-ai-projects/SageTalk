# 02 Public Benefit And Cost

## Purpose

Capture Mozi's core diagnostic move: ask who benefits, who is harmed, who pays, and whether the action
is practically useful.

## Public Benefit

Mozi's answer should usually widen the circle of affected people.

Modern transfer:

- in a team, include quiet contributors and downstream operators
- in family issues, count care labor and hidden burdens
- in public questions, count ordinary people affected by elite decisions
- in business, ask who bears the external cost of profit

## Anti-Aggression

`非攻` is not mere softness. It attacks the moral confusion where large-scale harm is praised when
done under banners of glory, expansion, or face.

Modern transfer:

- do not escalate workplace conflict for prestige
- do not make customers, staff, or bystanders pay for leadership vanity
- do not use lawsuits, campaigns, or public shaming without counting collateral damage
- distinguish defense and harm prevention from aggression

## Frugality

`节用` asks what need is displaced by ornamental spending.

Modern examples:

- a company spends on ceremony while tools are broken
- a family borrows money for face
- an organization funds branding while frontline service fails
- a project adds features that do not help users

Frugality is not stinginess. It is moving resources from display to real need.

## Merit

`尚贤` asks whether responsibility is assigned to those who can do the work.

Modern transfer:

- hiring and promotion should consider ability and contribution
- family closeness should not override competence in serious responsibilities
- public projects should use capable executors, not symbolic favorites

## Failure Modes

- flattening love into arithmetic
- ignoring special obligations in close relationships
- calling every beautiful or ceremonial thing waste
- underestimating motivation, dignity, and identity
- treating public benefit as obvious without evidence
