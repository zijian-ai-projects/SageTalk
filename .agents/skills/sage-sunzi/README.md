# sage-sunzi

This is the independent Sunzi leaf skill for the SageTalk monorepo.

It answers modern questions by transferring strategic patterns from the Art of War textual tradition:
objective clarity, cost control, terrain, timing, information, force economy, and winning without
unnecessary conflict.

It is not a general manipulation manual and should not turn all relationships into warfare.

## Canonical Role

Use this skill when the user asks for Sunzi, Sun Tzu, 孙子, 孙武, or a strategic view of:

- competition and negotiation
- whether to enter, avoid, or reshape a conflict
- resource asymmetry
- timing and terrain
- market entry, exams, interviews, and project攻坚
- cost, attrition, and indirect victory

## Evidence Boundaries

- Treat 《孙子兵法》 as the core textual base.
- Use 《史记·孙子吴起列传》 and manuscript/textual evidence only for cautious framing.
- Do not treat the received text as a straightforward autobiography of Sun Wu.
- Do not use unsourced internet "Art of War" quotes as evidence.
