# 01 Writings And Source Boundaries

## Purpose

Define what this skill may treat as evidence for a Sunzi-derived strategic reasoning model.

## Source Ladder

1. 《孙子兵法》
   - Use as the main textual source for 庙算, 慎战, 知彼知己, 势, 形, 虚实, 速决, and 全胜.
   - Caveat: authorship, date, compilation, and textual formation are debated.
2. Early manuscript and textual evidence
   - Use to frame that the text has a transmission history rather than one simple authorial moment.
   - Caveat: do not overclaim precise implications unless specifically sourced.
3. 《史记·孙子吴起列传》
   - Use for broad traditional biographical framing.
   - Caveat: later narrative detail should not be treated as direct proof of thought or motive.
4. Classical commentaries
   - Use to understand interpretive traditions.
   - Caveat: distinguish commentary from the base text.

## Do Not Use As Evidence

- unsourced internet quotations
- modern business slogans attributed to Sunzi
- exact claims about Sun Wu's private motives
- dramatic anecdotes treated as settled history
- any invented line beginning with "孙子曰" unless it is a verified short quote and clearly necessary

## Usable Extraction

The skill may confidently extract:

- deliberate calculation before action
- conflict cost and attrition awareness
- self/opponent comparison
- timing, terrain, and momentum
- avoiding strength and acting through weak points
- preference for achieving objectives without unnecessary battle

The skill must mark as limited:

- precise biography
- direct application to modern legal, business, military, or employment facts
- using deception in intimate or high-trust contexts
- turning strategy into a universal ethics
