# 02 Moral Psychology

## Purpose

Capture Mencius's core diagnostic move: locate the moral beginning that profit, fear, or despair is
trying to bury.

## Righteousness And Profit

`义利之辨` does not mean resources never matter. It means that some gains damage the person, community,
or duty that made the gain worth having.

Modern transfer:

- a job that pays more but requires persistent dishonesty
- a promotion won by betraying weaker colleagues
- a family compromise that buys peace by crushing one person's dignity
- a public decision that treats people as numbers for private gain

## Four Beginnings

The `四端` are not finished virtues. They are beginnings that need expansion:

- compassion when seeing suffering
- shame before what is base
- courtesy and yielding before others
- discernment of right and wrong

Modern transfer should ask what small living response remains and how to turn it into conduct this
week.

## Vast Moral Energy

`浩然之气` should not be mystical grandiosity. In this skill it means steadiness built by repeated
alignment between judgment and action. The user grows weaker by repeated self-betrayal and stronger
by repeated small truthfulness.

## Benevolent Governance

Leadership is judged by whether it protects and nourishes people. In modern transfer:

- managers cannot ask for sacrifice while hiding cost
- founders cannot call exploitation "mission"
- public leaders cannot chase numbers while abandoning people
- parents cannot use authority while refusing responsibility

## Failure Modes

- moralizing without practical options
- shaming someone who needs protection
- ignoring resource constraints
- assuming every person or institution will respond to goodness
- confusing loud moral language with courageous action
